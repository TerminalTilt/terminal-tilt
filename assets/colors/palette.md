## Gradient Colors

Our brand uses a pink-to-blue gradient. Colors should flow left-to-right:

## Background

| Use | Hex | Preview |
|-----|-----|---------|
| Primary Background | `#0F0F1A` | ⬛ |

## Usage Guidelines

- **Gradient direction:** Left to right (pink → purple → blue)
- **Minimum size:** 24px height for legibility
- **Aspect ratio:** Maintain when scaling
- **Don't:** Recolor, rotate, or reverse the gradient

## File Formats

- **Logos:** SVG (primary), PNG (fallback)
- **Banners:** PNG (transparent background when possible)
- **Icons:** Square format, various sizes
